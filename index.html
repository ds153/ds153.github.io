Biography

David Sathiaraj is a research professor in Data Science and Analytics in the Department of Geography and the Associate Director at the NOAA Southern Regional Climate Center (a Big Data center for enviornment informatics) at Louisiana State University in Baton Rouge, Louisiana, USA.

Research

My research is at the intersection of Computer Science and Big Data domains such as Environmental Informatics, Transportation, Socio-economics and Healthcare. Recent focus has been on deriving predictive analytics algorithms and applications for data-intensive domains such as Inland Water Transportation, Healthcare, Political Science and Climate Science. Work involves full-stack development of Big data acquisition systems, predictive algorithms that learn from growing data streams and development of web-based visual analytics for insight discovery.
